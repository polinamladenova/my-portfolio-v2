Polina Mladenova is a (budding) front-end developer based in Sofia, Bulgaria. With a background in learning and development, she decided to change career course and pursue her longtime love and appreciation for web design following (what in hindsight was) a very cathartic snowboarding trip with good friend and fellow SheCodes student Cynthia Alvez. Aside from her newfound love for coding (and writing in the third person?), Polina enjoys drawing, rock climbing and starting her morning off with a good espresso. As a developer, she aims to find simplicity to otherwise complex problems.
